Social Media Image Generator

This tool will add padding to your image and create a version optimized for social sharing (1200x630px).

Settings


Instructions:

  1. Click "Choose File" and select your Reflecting-01.png image
  2. Adjust padding and styling as needed
  3. Click "Generate Social Image"
  4. Click "Download Image" to save as Reflecting-01-social.png
  5. Upload the new image to your /public/images/ folder
  6. Update your blog post frontmatter to use the new image
